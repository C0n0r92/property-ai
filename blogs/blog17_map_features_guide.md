# Dublin Property Map: Navigate Dublin's Market Like a Pro

## Interactive Map Views

The **[Dublin Property Map](/map)** offers three distinct viewing modes:

- **Cluster View**: Properties grouped by proximity, showing market density and price ranges at a glance
- **Price View**: Individual markers displaying exact prices, revealing high-value areas and pricing patterns
- **Difference View**: Highlights bidding war hotspots where properties sell over asking price

**Data Sources**: Toggle between sold properties, current listings, and rental properties.
