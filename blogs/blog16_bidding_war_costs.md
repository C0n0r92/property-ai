# The True Cost of Dublin Bidding Wars: How Overpaying Today Costs You €200,000+ Tomorrow

## Executive Summary

Dublin's bidding wars drive property prices 10.7% above asking price on average (€102,753 premium), but this competitive fever comes at a devastating long-term cost. Analysis of 25,894 bidding war transactions reveals that a typical premium takes 21.8 years to break even through mortgage repayments alone, while adding €19,873-€52,996 in extra interest payments over 30 years. When factoring opportunity costs, the same €102,753 premium invested at 7% annual returns could grow to €202,131 in just 10 years - a €99,378 difference. This comprehensive analysis quantifies the true financial impact of Dublin's competitive property market.
