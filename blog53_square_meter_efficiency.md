# Dublin Square Meter Efficiency: Maximizing Space Value in Property Investment

## Executive Summary
Analysis of 13,977 Dublin property transactions reveals significant square meter efficiency variations across the market. D17 offers the most cost-effective space at €4,252 per square meter, while D9 commands €5,473 per square meter. Terrace properties demonstrate exceptional efficiency at €2,787 per square meter, with small apartments under 50 square meters costing €7,557 per square meter. ## Dublin's Space Value Landscape
Dublin property market exhibits significant variation in space efficiency, with buyers paying dramatically different amounts for equivalent square meters depending on location and property type. This analysis examines 13,977 Dublin properties sold between January 2024 and December 2025 to identify optimal value opportunities.

Square meter pricing varies by €1,222 across Dublin areas, representing a 28.8% efficiency differential between most and least efficient locations. D17 emerges as Dublin's most space-efficient area at €4,252 per square meter, followed by D24 at €4,298 and D22 at €4,385 per square meter.

**Top 5 Most Efficient Areas:**
