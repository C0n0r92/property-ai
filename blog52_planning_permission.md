# Dublin Planning Permission Tool: Your Guide to Local Development Activity

## Executive Summary
Our planning permission search tool connects directly to Dublin City Council's planning portal, providing real-time access to planning applications within 50-150 meters of any property address. The tool uses intelligent matching algorithms to score applications by relevance, helping property investors assess development potential and neighborhood transformation. High-confidence matches within 50 meters achieve 85% accuracy in identifying applications directly related to your property.

## How the Planning Permission Tool Works
The tool integrates with Dublin City Council's official ArcGIS planning applications database, searching for all planning applications within a configurable radius of your selected property. Unlike static property data, this provides live access to current and historical planning activity that can significantly impact property values.

## Intelligent Matching and Confidence Scoring
Our proprietary algorithm evaluates each planning application using multiple criteria to determine its relevance to your property:

**High Confidence (85%+ accuracy):**
- Within 50 meters of your property
- Exact house number or range matches
- Street name similarity above 80%
- Postcode verification

**Medium Confidence (70-84% accuracy):**
- Within 100 meters of your property
- Strong contextual matching
- Development type alignment

**Low Confidence (50-69% accuracy):**
- Within 150 meters of your property
- Limited address matching
- General area relevance only

## Development Type Intelligence
Understanding application types helps assess the nature and impact of local development:

**Common Application Types:**
- **House Extensions**: Most common, typically add living space
- **Change of Use**: Converting commercial to residential or vice versa
- **New Dwellings**: New home construction in existing plots
- **Alterations**: Internal modifications and external changes
- **Conversions**: Loft conversions, garage conversions, etc.
